Hurricane

before...


A hurricane is a rapidly rotating storm system characterized by a low-pressure center, a closed low-level atmospheric circulation, strong winds, and a spiral arrangement of thunderstorms that produce heavy rain.

  • Bring in outdoor furniture
  • Disconnect electrical appliances
  • Turn off gas and electricity

Take Action!

  • Move immediately to higher ground
  • Evacuate if directed
  • Build your emergency prepared kit

Hurricane

during...


A hurricane is a rapidly rotating storm system characterized by a low-pressure center, a closed low-level atmospheric circulation, strong winds, and a spiral arrangement of thunderstorms that produce heavy rain.

  • Stay Inside
  • Leave all electrical items unplugged
  • Turn off gas and electricity
  • Move to the highest point in your home
  • Evacuate if directed

Hurricane

after...


A hurricane is a rapidly rotating storm system characterized by a low-pressure center, a closed low-level atmospheric circulation, strong winds, and a spiral arrangement of thunderstorms that produce heavy rain.

  • Drain Excess Water Around House
  • Identify Any Potential Leaks
  • Get Professional Help if There is Flooding in your House!
